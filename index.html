Bo Zhang

 

I am a Research Staff Member in the Mathematical Sciences group of the IBM T.J. Watson Research Center. I am a co-chair of the Reasoning PIC (Professional Interest Community) and the chair of the Operations Research PIC at the Watson Research Center. You can contact me at bozhang@gatech.edu or zhangbo@us.ibm.com.

I have held visiting scholar or adjunct faculty positions with Columbia University, New York University, the U.S. Federal Reserve Board, National University of Singapore, Bell Labs, and the Center for Mathematics and Computer Science in the Netherlands. I served on the committee responsible for writing and reviewing the very first set of exam questions for the Certified Analytics Professional program, the world's first general analytics certification program.
